"The Lord has been my strength and guide through every trial..."

Born and raised in a Christian family in India, my personal relationship with Jesus deepened through trials and experiences. From overcoming fear and stress with the promises of Scripture to trusting Him through a severe accident, I've seen God's faithfulness at every turn.

My Ministry Calling

Today, my ministry focuses on sharing the hope of Christ with international students like me. Having faced challenges as a student navigating new environments, I am passionate about helping others find their identity and purpose in Christ.

Inspired by the Great Commission, I serve with the goal of guiding international students to know Jesus, experience His love, and walk confidently in His plan for their lives.

Bridges Ministry

I'm actively involved with Bridges International, a ministry that exists "to see every international student wholeheartedly follow Jesus, both on campus and around the world."

Our Vision

Growing genuine communities of international students who welcome seekers and help each other to follow Jesus.

Our Approach

We empower international students to lead in ministry, reach their classmates with the gospel, and grow in Christ throughout their time in the U.S.

International Students, Inc. (ISI)

I serve with ISI, a ministry dedicated to reaching international students with the love of Christ and connecting them with local Christians.

ISI provides opportunities for friendship, cultural exchange, and spiritual growth for students far from home.
