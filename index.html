"The Lord has been my strength and guide through every trial..."

Born and raised in a Christian family in India, my personal relationship with Jesus deepened through trials and experiences. From overcoming fear and stress with the promises of Scripture to trusting Him through a severe accident, I've seen God's faithfulness at every turn.

My journey has led me to minister to international students who face similar challenges.

MY CALLING

My Ministry Calling

Today, my ministry focuses on sharing the hope of Christ with international students like me. Having faced challenges as a student navigating new environments, I am passionate about helping others find their identity and purpose in Christ.
